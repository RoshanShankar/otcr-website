About Us

OTCR is comprised of 60 top students, primarily from the Colleges of Engineering and Business, and has advised clients ranging in size from small businesses to Fortune 50 corporationns in a variety of industries and practices for over 15 years.

OTCR's mission is to:

Deliver outstanding solutions to clients for their unique business challenges

Accelerate learning in a high-energy environment to prepare our students to become leaders in a variety of different careers

Create a collaborative environment for the collective growth of students at UIUC
